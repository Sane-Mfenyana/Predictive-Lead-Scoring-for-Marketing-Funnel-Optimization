The Challenge

A financial institution's telemarketing campaign suffers from inefficient lead prioritization. With a baseline conversion rate of only 11.3%, sales agents waste time on low-potential leads while high-value opportunities are missed. The marketing team needs a data-driven way to understand the customer journey, predict which leads are most likely to convert, and monitor campaign performance in real-time to optimize spend.

Explore the Project

This end-to-end project demonstrates the full commercial analytics lifecycle:

  • Data Engineering: SQL in Google BigQuery for data discovery and funnel definition.
  • Data Science: Python for EDA, feature engineering, and building Logistic Regression models (AUC: 0.80 operational, 0.94 diagnostic).
  • Business Intelligence: An interactive Tableau dashboard with lead prioritization, conversion drivers, and performance analytics.
  • Business Translation: Model coefficients converted into strategic recommendations for marketing and sales.
